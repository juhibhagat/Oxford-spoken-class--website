30-Day Spoken English Challenge.

This Course will be completed in just 30 Days and it is possible For any Person who can at-least read and write in English.

Course Highlights:

  • In this Course 3 Levels are Covered, they are Basic Level, Advanced Level & Professional Levels.
  • We cover Speech Rules, Public speaking, Presentation Skills, Body Language, Etiquette, Self Awareness, Personality Development, Writing Skills, Vocabulary, Pronunciations, Group Discussions, Debates, Role Plays, Dialogues, JAM, Interview skills, Resume Building, E-MAIL writing....
  • Speech Rules - In order to speak mistake free one should have complete awareness of when, where, why and how to use the words in framing correct sentence. Speech Rules help us improve our command over the language and speak mistake free English.
  • Public Speaking - Helps you overcome your stage fear & improves your self confidence, makes you a confident public speaker..
  • Personality Development Sessions - These Sessions help you realize your weaknesses and Strengths, lets you analyze your self..
  • Group Discussions - Helps you understand the importance of G.D's in any MNC & helps you understand the do's and don'ts in it. It also helps you become a good Team player / Team Lead / Team member.
  • Debates - Helps you how to express your point of view to others who disagree with your opinion and lets to know the tricks and methods to convince the other person / persons.
